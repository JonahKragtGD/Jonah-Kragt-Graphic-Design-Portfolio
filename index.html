My Graphic Design Portfolio

Over the years I have had the amazing opportunity to work on many projects. From personal passion projects to freelance or professional work, I have a wide range of products that have helped shape me as a designer. I am always learning and improving, so feedback and criticizm is welcome.

Design Challenges

Design challenges are something I do in order to keep my skill sharp using prompts from the website sharpen. This allows me to get a random prompt to work on in a similar vein to a design job. I love this website and it has many different prompts.

Logos

Logo design is one of my favorite things in the design world. I love the challenge that comes with creating something recognizable, iconic, and reduceable. During my time as a professional desinger, an intern, and as a student, logo design has always been one of my favorite aspects of graphic design as a whole.

Posters

Posters are one of the first things people think about when they hear "graphic design" poster design is something I find perfect for fan projects on my favorite media. You see them everywhere so why not here?

YouTube Thumbnails

During the COVID pandemic I was stuck inside like everyone else. I decided to use that time to start up a YouTube channel I had made when I was in high school. I took my knowledge I had gained as a student at Iowa State and turned it into a YouTube page. It was a fun way to keep busy and a great way to keep my skills sharp.

Social Media Graphics

During my time as an intern with the World Food Prize Foundation I had the opportunity to create some graphics that they posted to their various social channels. It was a fun way to get familiar with the various websites and understand their size and dimentions.
